Waiting For Godot - The Video Game ¶

This is a work of genius from the people behind the webcomic site VectorBelly. You can also download and play the game, though it has been altered because, as the makers note, it has not been so well received by the copyright holders of Beckett’s works:

There was an awesome game here, but due to a cease and desist letter, I had to change the name and premise of it.

This is a real shame, but Beckett fans will still enjoy the exciting gameplay and soundtrack even in altered form.  I really want to see what 2P mode is like …
